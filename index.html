About Us

            Welcome to Team 11's sandwich making website! Our website is built to allow our users to express their creativity in something that 
            everyone loves, food! Food is something that brings people together and that is our goal, to bring people together through the art
            of crafting their sandwich masterpiece.

Ingredients:

            Click on the ingredients tab to check out the available ingredients! There you will be able to see the nutrition facts about the
            different ingredients. We want our users to make the healthiest choices to create the sandwich that makes them the happiest.

Build Your Sanwich:

            Click on the Build Your Sandwich tab to begin designing! You can either type the ingredients you want to have in your sanwich in 
            our input box, or drag the ingredients over to the plate where your sandwich is displayed. Ingredients can be placed in any order
            so be creative!
